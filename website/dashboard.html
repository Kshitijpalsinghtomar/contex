Security & Data Integrity

Every Contex-encoded payload carries cryptographic proof of provenance ‚Äî non-tamperable fingerprints and HMAC-SHA256 watermarks that make the pipeline computationally infeasible to replicate.

Structural Fingerprinting & Anti-Copy Watermarking 5-Layer Protection
1
Entropy-Weighted Field Ordering ‚Äî Fields sorted by Shannon entropy distribution, not just alphabetically. Replicating exact ordering requires knowledge of the full entropy profile.
2
Salted Structural Hash Chain ‚Äî Each pipeline stage's output salts the next stage's hash. Breaking one link invalidates the entire chain downstream.
3
Pipeline-State Fingerprint ‚Äî IR metadata embeds the full pipeline state: canonicalization parameters, dictionary configuration, and tokenizer version.
4
Structural Complexity Scoring ‚Äî Entropy + graph metrics produce a 0‚Äì100 score classifying data as trivial ‚Üí simple ‚Üí moderate ‚Üí complex ‚Üí extreme.
5
Encoding Watermark (HMAC-SHA256) ‚Äî Invisible byte-level markers in binary output. generateWatermark() creates them, verifyWatermark() proves provenance.
